# GBPJPY Range-to-Expansion Core Principle

Date: 2026-07-11
Status: `[LOCKED_RESEARCH_PRINCIPLE]`

## Core idea

GBPJPY may remain in consolidation for hours or several days, but consolidation is not the final state. Over a sufficiently long horizon, price eventually leaves the range and expands in one direction.

The research objective is not to predict the eventual direction in advance. It is to identify the transition from:

`Range -> Transition -> Trend`

and capture the confirmed middle portion of the expansion.

## Important distinction

This principle does not mean every first breakout is genuine.

GBPJPY can:

- remain in a range longer than expected;
- produce multiple false breaks on both sides;
- leave the range but fail to travel far enough after costs;
- reverse after an apparently valid breakout.

Therefore, the setup must wait for evidence of acceptance outside the range.

## Research consequences

The GBPJPY system must measure and test:

1. Range duration and width.
2. Bollinger Band Width compression percentile.
3. 20MA slope and distance from 200MA.
4. Repeated crossings of 20MA and 200MA during consolidation.
5. Reactions around quarter levels: 00 / 25 / 50 / 75.
6. Number and direction of false breaks before the real expansion.
7. M15 close and follow-through outside the range.
8. M1 retest acceptance and structure confirmation.
9. Remaining movement after confirmation.
10. Opportunity Extraction Rate: captured pips divided by the executable movement remaining after confirmation.

## Timeframe structure

- D1: broad directional environment and major compression/expansion cycle.
- H4: primary directional bias and regime.
- H1: trend, pullback, Fibonacci and important support/resistance.
- M15: Range / Transition / Trend classification and setup confirmation.
- M1: precise entry, invalidation and campaign management.
