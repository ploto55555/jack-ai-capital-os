# Checkpoint 002 — Ketty Regime Classifier V0.1

Date: 2026-07-10
Status: `[COMPLETED RESEARCH STAGE]`

## Objective

Test whether Ketty's trend/range/unclear market classification improves GBPJPY strategy quality enough to support the USD 500 to USD 100,000 rolling 12-month research objective.

## Data used

- GBPJPY H1, 2010-06-24 to 2026-07-08
- GBPJPY H4, 2010-06-24 to 2026-07-08
- Supplied spread-point column included
- Next-bar-open execution
- One position at a time
- Stop-first assumption when stop and target are touched in the same bar

## Research completed

- 300 trend-pullback combinations
- 144 initial trend-breakout combinations
- 72 range mean-reversion combinations
- 576 trend-breakout combinations across six regime-classifier variants
- In-sample / validation / out-of-sample segmentation
- Calendar-year analysis
- 190 rolling 12-month capital windows
- Risk levels from 0.5% to 10% per trade

## Confirmed findings

### Rejected modules

- Ketty range mean reversion V0.1: negative full-history result.
- Ketty trend pullback V0.1: no combination remained positive in all three periods.

### Research candidate retained

`KETTY_GBPJPY_TREND_BREAKOUT_V0.1`

Rules:

- H4 uptrend: close above SMA20, normalized SMA14 slope above +0.03, close above the shifted Ichimoku cloud.
- H4 downtrend: inverse conditions.
- H1 entry: completed close breaks the previous 40-bar high/low and candle body is at least 0.3 ATR.
- Entry: next H1 open.
- Stop: 1.5 ATR.
- Target: 5R.
- Maximum hold: 96 H1 bars.

Full-history result:

- 1,246 trades
- Win rate 21.27%
- Profit Factor 1.1385
- Expectancy +0.1089R
- Total +135.69R
- Maximum drawdown 37.01R

Out-of-sample 2023-2026:

- 274 trades
- Profit Factor 1.0677
- Expectancy +0.0539R
